How to add data sets to Master Item (Measure) ?

First Lets Create a Measure on Master Items using the Following the steps below.

Measure:  Average Total Payments

next

Next Copy and paste "$(vAvgCoveredCharges)" on the screen as shown below.


Lastly, follow as shown below to save the measure.

Now lets add the other Measures Starting with "Average Total Payments"

Copy and paste the  fx expression =: "$(vAverageTotalPayments)"


Measure:  "Discharge"

Copy and paste the  fx expression =: "$(vDischarges)"



Measures: "Avg Out of Pocket Percentage"

Copy and paste the  fx expression =: "Avg([Avg Out of Pocket Percentage])"

Click "Create" to Save.


Measures: "Avg Ins Cover Percentage"

Copy and paste the  fx expression =: "Avg([Avg Ins Cover Percentage])"

Click "Create" to Save.



lastly,

Measures: "Charge Paid%"

Copy and paste the  fx expression =: "(((Avg([Average Total Payments]))/(Avg([Average Covered Charges]))))"

Click "Create" to Save.
